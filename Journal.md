    - General Overview: This project was miraculously completed in one day! After a generational lock in streak, I was able to go through everything, from the initial planning stages, to even testing the firmware on the microcontroller. I decided to follow the clean minimal asthetic I prefer with my other hardware projects, and focused heavily on portability, with the case. The physical footprint of the pcb is also pretty small, and I was able to give is a nice unique shape to it. I initially was planning to use a bigger RP Pico 2 but realized the Seeed-Studio XIAO rp2040 is a much better choice, due to its size.
